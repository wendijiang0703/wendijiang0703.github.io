About

Hi! I’m Wendi Jiang — a designer from China, currently based in London, where I’m pursuing a Bachelor’s degree in Graphic Communication Design at Central Saint Martins, University of the Arts London.

I explore the sweet spot where creativity, technology, and human connection collide. My work often blends physical fabrication with digital processes. Whether it’s coding robotic experiences, collaborating with AI, or experimenting with non-planar printing, I’m all about pushing the boundaries of how we experience design.

When I’m not designing, you’ll probably find me walking the runway, volunteering at a charity shop, or sitting in a theatre. Let’s make something unforgettable!
