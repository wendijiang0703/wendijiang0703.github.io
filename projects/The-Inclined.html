The Inclined

product

Physical Simulation and Parametric-Designed Core for The Leaning Tower of Pisa

Workshop outcome at Tongji University's Digital Futures

Teammates: Chen Cheng, Liang Zelin, Xu Ruijie, Yu Zelong

Shanghai, China

2024.7

#topology optimization #3d printing

The Leaning Tower of Pisa was a dangerous building when it was built in 1372 , and for the last 8 centuries, people have tried to straighten it in various ways. By utilizing the topology optimization platform Ameba to calculate and generate form and structure, this project aims to protect the heritage of the Leaning Tower of Pisa by reimagining its core tube. The supporting structure counteracts leaning forces and prevents the tower from leaning further.
