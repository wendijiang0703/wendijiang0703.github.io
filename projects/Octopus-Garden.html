Octopus’ Garden

2020 · product

30cm*30cm*15cm

Paper, UV Curable Resin, tracing paper, candy

I love all forms of art, including poetry. The theme of the work is the collection of my poetries, and the specific presentation is a book. The content is divided into three parts. The largest part of the outermost paper is collages, and all the collage materials used here were shot during my trips by myself. In the middle is illustrations of my poems. The smallest inter part, is the complete text of my poems. The three layers of diminishing design presents a microcosm of my inner world.
