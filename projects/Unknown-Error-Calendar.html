Unknown Error Calendar

2022 · ux / graphic

Unknow error calendar is a set of design. The ordinary and tragic life of an ordinary man is recounted in 12 concrete examples. The audience can feel the uncertainty and pessimism of life in this design, but at the same time it reminds them that luck does not come easily.
