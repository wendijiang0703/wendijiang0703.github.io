The Hug

product

Concrete Casted Assembly

#3d printing#casting#parametric design

2024.5

In this parametric design project, I utilized Grasshopper to create a parametric mesh that can be modifiable by nine variables such as location of points, strength and load. I then designed a set of structures featuring a joint structure in Rhino. Using these designs, I have SLA 3D printed a resin mold for casting. The final outcome is a small-scale concrete structure, showcasing the seamless integration of parametric design and practical fabrication.
