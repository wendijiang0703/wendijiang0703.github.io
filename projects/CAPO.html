CAPO

2022 · ux

Capo is a typeface I created after being inspired from an object. A capo is originally a device a musician uses on the neck of a stringed (typically fretted) instrument to transpose and shorten the playable length of the strings—hence raising the pitch. It is a common tool for players of guitars. I started my design from the metal Capo that I found in my room because I was drawn to its perfect curvature and metallic sheen. To me this font is dynamic and intriguing.
