Warping Time

2023 · graphic

Warping Time is a coding artwork that inspired by I usually feel anxious when time fly. The clock represents the fluidity and subjectivity of time, and how it can be warped and manipulated. The digital clock also features an interactive element where the mouse can be used to manipulate the clock's hands, emphasizing the idea of the subjective nature of time.

Overall, Warping Time invites the viewer to question their relationship with time and the concept of its fixed nature, encouraging them to embrace the fluidity and malleability of this fundamental element our existence.
