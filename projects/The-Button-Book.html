The Button Book

2025 · graphic

The printed companion to The Button installation — a 'Button Of The Day' zine documenting the everyday buttons I encountered during research. Each spread pairs a photo of a button-in-the-wild with the time and place it was found, building an archive of overlooked everyday interfaces.
