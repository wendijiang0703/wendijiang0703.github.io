An Unfair Game

2022

An unfair game is a design of board game, which including the map, cards, characters and packaging. Board games are a way for people to escape from the real world, where it is relatively fair. So, what happens if the game doesn't care about fairness? What if a game’s ending is already decided at the beginning? I want to use my project “an unfair game” to make people aware of the situation of the female as underprivileged in society. My board game will have some design in a logical way to make the game become unfair for different characters that players had chosen from the beginning and lead them to go into my designed ending. The player thought they are playing a board game but the ending was already clear when they chose the gender of characters.
