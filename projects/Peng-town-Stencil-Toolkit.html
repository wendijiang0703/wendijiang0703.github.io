Peng town Stencil Toolkit

2023

Peng town is an ancient town located near my city which originally built during the Yongle period of the Ming Dynasty (1403-1424). With its grey-tiled roofs, white walls, and brick and wooden buildings, this town has a unique character that transports you back in time.

During my visit to Peng town over the Easter break of 2023, I was struck by the peaceful and leisurely lifestyle of its residents. The town is full of old teahouses, both large and small, have preserved the essence of ancient Chinese culture, making Peng town a true gem of China’s heritage.

Inspired by my visit, I have designed the Peng town stencil toolkit, a collection of figures that I captured with my lens during my stay. As a tribute to the town’s rich cultural heritage, by use of these stencils, anyone can bring a piece of Peng town’s unique character into their life, and experience the tranquillity and slow pace of life that this town embodies.
