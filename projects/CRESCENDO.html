CRESCENDO

2020 · product

Crescendo makes it easy to create experimental music by processing the sounds of life, and the visual remix makes it fun to create music even if you have no musical background. You can simply create and listen to sounds from all over the world, and you can also make friends with your music. For user, all the operations in the app are free, so you can give full play to your creativity and let your work be heard by all the people around the world.
