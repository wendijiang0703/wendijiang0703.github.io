Big Ear

2022 · product

In this project I have examined the form and meaning of everyday objects. I explored semiotics and investigated what makes an “archetypal” object, considering how they are used to convey narrative in design and image making.

After using only paper to made a graphic big ear, I have also remade this project into a more realistic style by painting the ear and photographing it in different locations and scenarios.
