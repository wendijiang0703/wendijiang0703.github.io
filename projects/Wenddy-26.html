Wenddy*26

2023 · graphic

This captivating project, a collaborative endeavor between myself and AI entity Midjourney, revolves around the theme of “Beautiful Errors.” Through a series of 26 mesmerizing images, I morph my own likeness into various historical street scenes from around the world. These images, accompanied by enigmatic text, exist in a realm where meaning and content blur, creating a captivating juxtaposition of visual and linguistic elements.
