Women in The Courtauld Gallery

2022 · ux / graphic

Women in The Courtauld Gallery is a project about data visualization, I have using the presence of women in museums as a theme, I have specifically collected a set of data with a subjective focus, including the proportion of female artists and the inclusion of female figures in the works of the gallery, the gender ratio of visitors, and the gender ratio of consumers in the gallery. The booklet is intended to make people aware of the presence of the male gaze in the art history and to reflect on it.
