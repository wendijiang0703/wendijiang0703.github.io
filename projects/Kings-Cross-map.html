Kings Cross Map

2023 · ux

The brief of context is about the radical potential of observation and movement. We been ask to based on how we can engaged with local and surrounding area, to create a map to response to it.

I live, study and work in KX. I live in the Kings Cross area for my university. I am a BA students study graphic communication design in CSM. I also work as volunteers every weekend in Boutique by Shelter at Coal Drops Yard and do Pilates in FRAME kings cross almost every day. In that case, I spend most of daily time in the neighborhood where I loved so much. In this project, I had made a pattern collection based on the memory I had in this lovely community.
