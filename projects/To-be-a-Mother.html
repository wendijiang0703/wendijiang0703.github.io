To be a Mother

2022 · graphic

To be a Mother is a installation and interactive work, and I have specifically present it as a video of performance. It is not easy to build up a installation like that, I have used the process of building different fragments to metaphor how difficult for a woman to balance different role that society has expected them to be a mother.
