Both Sides Now

2024 · ux / product

(Group work with Cheng Zhao and Arthur Yu)

If you’ve ever been a tourist, anywhere, it’s hard to suppress the feeling of being frowned upon, to dream that you knew the streets, and the language inside out. But for someone finding themselves at home, what they’ve been missing is someone to refresh how they’ve looked at what they’re accustomed to see. We introduce Both Sides Now as a way of make these connections, between a tourist (Traveller) and a local (Guide) tangible, memorable, and everlasting. As a part of Airbnb, we hope it makes the act of being there for someone a enriching experience for yourself.
