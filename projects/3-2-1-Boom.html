3, 2, 1... Boom!

2025 · graphic

(Group work with Cheng Zhao and Aolei Kang)

Our identity takes inspiration from fireworks and confetti, reimagining celebration through the lens of sustainability. We collected off-cuts from the art school—materials that carry the traces of every discipline—and transformed them into vibrant forms of expression. Using only Riso print colors and a zero-waste process, the design embodies critical making, exchange, and transformation. It celebrates the collective spirit of CSM, where every fragment contributes to a radiant whole that speaks to students across all courses.
