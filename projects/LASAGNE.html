LASAGNE

2022 · ux

We were asked to develop a modular typeface. Using rectangular pieces of insulation tape, I came up with a design that reminded me of sheets of Lasagne.

I experimented with actual lasagne as a material, and made a lasercut Perspex box for packaging.
